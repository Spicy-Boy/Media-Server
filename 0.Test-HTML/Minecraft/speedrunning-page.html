RULES

Accepted Versions: Super BTW or BTW CE 2.1.4.. You may use Ears, Neodymium, Random Things, and Dynamic Lighting addons

1. Your run is complete when you pull the diamond 💎💎💎 pickaxe off the crafting table.

2. 🌎 You must start from a fresh world. Dying and respawning is okay.

3. You must use a random seed during world creation. 🌱

4. Survival mode only. No using creative mode, no teleporting, no giving yourself items, no looking up coordinates or map information in any way. 💀 Cheating is for the weak.

5. You must record 📹 your run and post it online! Record world creation.

6. Given the long nature of BTW gameplay, you may pause your run. 🐛🐛 Use the /time command at the end of segments and the beginning of new segments to show continuity. Do not play on your world between recordings or face disqualification.

6+(1/2). Exiting and rejoining your world to reset the AI of mobs in your world will result in your run being disqualified.

7. ⏲️ Run time ⏲️ can be kept with an external timer. Video time can also be used to tell the length of your run, so record the whole thing.

8. The SPECIAL prize will be given by community vote. Community members will vote for the user that constructs the coolest or best looking mob trap within their competition world. Players wishing to participate in this can continue to play after crafting the diamnond pick, if they so wish!
